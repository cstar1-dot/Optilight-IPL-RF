Dual-Energy Technology

Two Groundbreaking Technologies.
One Powerful Protocol.

💡

OptiLight IPL

Lumenis OptiLight is the first and only FDA-approved Intense Pulsed Light therapy specifically indicated for dry eye disease due to MGD. Precisely calibrated light pulses target the inflammatory cascade driving gland dysfunction — reducing abnormal blood vessel growth, eliminating Demodex mites, and thermally activating clogged glands.

  • Targets inflammatory mediators at the source
  • Reduces telangiectasia feeding eyelid inflammation
  • Eliminates Demodex mite overpopulation — a hidden MGD trigger
  • Stimulates healthy lipid secretion from glands
  • FDA-approved with robust clinical trial evidence
  • Proven improvement in TBUT, OSDI scores & meibum quality
⚡

Radiofrequency (RF)

Our advanced Radiofrequency technology delivers precisely controlled thermal energy into eyelid tissue, producing deep collagen remodeling and powerful gland expression. RF elevates tissue temperature to therapeutically melt and mobilize inspissated (hardened) meibum — clearing blocked glands and restoring oil flow to your tear film.

  • Heats Meibomian glands from within to clear blockages
  • Stimulates collagen & elastin production in periorbital tissue
  • Improves eyelid laxity — a key structural MGD contributor
  • Enhances gland morphology and prevents progressive atrophy
  • Reaches temperatures conventional warm compresses cannot
  • Synergistic anti-inflammatory effects that amplify IPL results
